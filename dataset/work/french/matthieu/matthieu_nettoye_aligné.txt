Angakiril a Yesu Kristu
1 ‘Minam mawiy majin ma angakiril a Yesu Kristu, wa a majuku ma David, niyend David wading wa a majuku ma Aburaham.
2 Aburaham wamuvala Isak, Isak wamuvala Jakob, ni Jakob wamuvala Yuda ni anamakwend.
3 Yuda wamuvala Peres ni Zerak, wayivala nend Tamar. Peres wamuvala Hesiron ni Hesiron wamuvala Ram.
4 Ram wamuvala Aminadab, Amina- dab wamuvala Nakishon, ni Nakishon wamuvala Solomon.
5 Solomon wamuvala Bowaz, wamuvala nend Rahab. Bowaz wamuvala Obed, wamuvala nend Rut. Obed wamuvala Yese
6 Yese wamuvala Mwant David, David wamuvala Salomon, wamuvala nend mband wa Uria.
7 Salomon wamuvala Robowam, Robowam wamuvala Abija, ni Abija wamuvala Asa.
8 Asa wamuvala Josafat, Josafat wamu- vala Joram, ni Joram wamuvala Ozias.
9 Ozias wamuvala Jotam Jotam wamuvala Akaz, ni Akaz wamuvala Ezekias.
10 Ezekias wamuvala Manase, Manase wamuvala Amon, ni Amon wamu- vala Yosias.
11 Yosias wamuvala Jekonias ni anamakwend pa chisu chadingau ‘in Isarel mu winkwal ku Babilon.
12 Padiokalau in Isarel mu winkwal ku Babilon, Jekonias wamuvala Sheyalitiel, ni Sheyalitiel wamuvala Zorobabel.
13 Zorobabel wamuvala Abiud, Abiud wamuvala Eliakim, ni Eliakim wamuvala Azor.
14 Azor wamuvala Sadok, Sadok wamuvala Akim, ni Akim wamuvala Eliud.
15 Eliud wamuvala Eleyazar, Eleyazar wamuvala Matan, ni Matan wamuvala Jakob.
16 Jakob wamuvala Josef, ikundj wa Mariy, makwend a Yesu, upidilau anch, Kristu Uzizidilau Many.
17 Chawiy kudiokal ku Aburaham djat ni ku David kwading majuku dikum ni manying. Kudiokal ku David djat ni payitwalau in Isarel mu winkwal ku Babilon, kwading majuku dikum ni manying. 19 Josef wading muntu utentamina, kasotap kumu- kwatish Mariy usany pakach pa antu. Chad ndiy warijeka mutapu wa kumulik ku ujindj.
20 Pachadingay ni kutongijok mulong wa chom chinech, muruu wa mwiur wa Chinawej weza kudi ndiy mu chilat, wamwel anch: “Josef, wa a majuku ma David, kangal wov wom wa kumwat mukajey Mariy, mulong ndiy wimisha ku ukash wa Mwiku Utumbila.
21 Ndiy ukez kuval mwan ikundj, chad ey ukez kumupil anch Yesu, mulong ndiy ukez kuyipandish antwend ku yitil yau.”
22 Chom chinech chamekana mulong wa kuwanyish mazu ma Chinawej mamulejay muruu wend anch:
23 “Changadim wakad kumwijik ikundj ukez kwimish, chad ukez kuval mwan ikundj ukez kupilau anch, Emanuwel, charumburik anch, Nzamb udi ni yetwetu.”
24 Chalankay Josef pa tul, ndiy wasala mudi mwamulejau kudi muruu wa mwiur, ndiy wamwata mukajend Mariy.

1 Yesu wavadika mu ul wa Betlehem mwi ngand ya Yudeya, pa chirung chadingay Herod mwant. Pa chisu chinicha in manangu amwing ilejina yom ya atutumbu, adiokala kwingangel, ez ku Yerusalem.
2 Awiy ipula anch: “Ov, kwisu kwavadikinay mwan ukeza kwikal mwant wa in Yuda? Etu twamen katumbu wend udiokil kwingangel, chad twezin mulong wa kumwifukwil.”
3 Chovay Herod rusangu riner, kamanap kand mwakutwa- dil, pamwing ni antu awonsu a mu Yerusalem.
4 Ndiy wayikumangeja in kupesh ajim awonsu ni alej a Yijil, wayipul anch: “Ov, kwisu kukuvadikay Kristu Uzizidilau Many?”
5 Awiy amwakula anch: “Ukuvadikin mu ul wa Betlehem mwi ngand ya Yudeya, mulong muruu wa Nzamb wafunda anch:
6 ‘Ey, Betlehem, mwi ngand ya Yuda, kiudiap kakemp kupandakanamu misumb yawonsu ya mu Yuda. Mukach mwa antwey nikez kumubudish mwin kutakel wa antwam in Isarel.’ ”
7 Chawiy Herod wayitazuka ku ujindj in manangu adio- kala kwingangel, wayipompish ni kushinshik chakwel amu- leja kamu chisu chamekanay katumbu.
8 Chad ndiy wayituma ku Betlehem, wayilej anch: “Yangany, ukamusurabany mwan. Anch wamumanany nlejanyamu, chakwel niya kand ni kumwifukwil.”
9 Chadiokalau kudi mwant, awila mwi njil, chad amumana katumbu wamanau kwingangel. Katumbu wayitakela djat ni pimanay pa ndond padingay mwan.
10 Awiy asangara nakash kupandakan pamumanau kand katumbu.
11 Andama mu chiku- mbu, amuman mwan ni makwend Mariy. Chad asa manungun pansh, amwifukwil mwan. Aburila mu yipau yau, abudish yom ya matangal, utad wa or, ni diseng dia malash, ni malash ma rupwembu ruwamp, chad amwinkish mwan.
12 Chad Nzamb wayibabesha mu chilat chakwel kangal achirik kwa Herod. Awiy asutila mwi njil yikwau, achirik kwol kwau. Akat kuchinyin mu Idjibit
13 Chayau in manangu, muruu wa mwiur wa Chinawej wamekana kudi Josef mu chilat. Wamubabesha anch: “Herod ukez kumukimb mwan mulong wa kumujip. Chawiy tumbuk, yangany ni mwan ni makwend, uchinyinany mwi ngand ya Idjibit. Ukashakamany mwinimwa djat ni pinikez kukulejany kudiokalamu.”
14 Uchuku wawiy winiwa, Josef walanka, wamusend mwan ni makwend, aya ku Idjibit.
15 Awiy ashakama kwini- kwa djat ni pafilay Herod. Chom chinech chamekana mulong wa kuwanyish mazu malonday Chinawej kudi muruu wend anch: “Namutazuka Mwanam kudiokal ku Idjibit.” Akat kuyijip an
16 Chijikay Herod anch, in manangu adiokala kwingangel, amudimbil kal, ndiy wajimana nakash. Ndiy walejana kuyijip an amakundj awonsu a mu Betlehem ni mu makunku ma piswimp avadika pa chisu chinicha, kushik ni ku an akweta mivu yaad. Ndiy wasala yom yiney mulong wa yom yateshay kudi in manangu, pa chisu chamekanay katumbu.

18 “Dipumb diovakana mu Rama, dipumb dia madil makash. Rechel ukat kudil mulong wa anend. Ulikin kumwongoj, mulong anend afa kal.” Akat kudiokal ku Idjibit

20 Wamuleja anch: “Lank, musenda mwan ni makwend, uchirik nau mwi ngand ya Isarel, mulong adia asotila kumujip mwan afil kwau.”
21 Chad Josef walanka, wamusend mwan ni makwend, wachirik nau mwi ngand ya Isarel.
22 Pakwez Josef, chateshay anch, Arkelaus wamuswan kal tatukwend Herod kwikal mwant wa ngand ya Yudeya, ndiy wova wom wa kuya kwinikwa. Nzamb wamubabesha Josef mu chilat, chad ndiy waya mwi ngand ya Galalia.

5 Antu adiokalanga ku Yerusalem, ni mwi ngand yawonsu ya Yudeya, ni mwi ngand yawonsu yadinga piswimp ni wit wa Yordan, ezanga kudi ndiy.

9 Kangal walamiti- nany kulond anch: ‘Aburaham udi nshankur wetu.’ Chakin kamu, nakulejany anch, Nzamb ukutwish kukarumun mayal minam kwikal antu a a majuku ma Aburaham! 
10 Kasok kalel kwishin kwa mitond mulong wa kukatul mij. Chawiy mutond wawonsu wakad kusong mabuj mawamp, akez kuukatul, audjibul mu kasu.
11 Am nakubatishany kal nich mem mulong wa kumekesh anch, wakarumukany kal kudiokal ku yitil yen. Pakwez muntu ukweza kwinyim kwam ukez kukubatishany ni Mwiku Utumbila nich kasu. Ndiy wimpandakenamu am. Am kinidiap muwamp uwanyidina ap kusend yisapat yend.
12 Ndiy ukwet kal ral mu makas mend, chakwel ukutwish kupep mulong wa kwauril mbut ni yipapal. Ukez kukumangej mbut mwi nkil yend, pakwez yipapal ukez kuyosh mu kasu kakad kujimang.” Akat kumubatish Yesu

16 Chamubatishau Yesu, ndiy watuntuka mu mem. Pawiy kamu pinipa diur diamwanzurikina ndiy, chad wamana Mwiku wa Nzamb wading mudi iyemb wamushirikina.
