# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making **Lugayetu** a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment for everyone.  
This project brings together developers, researchers, linguists, and native speakers working toward a common goal: preserving and developing technologies for African languages.

All participants are expected to contribute in a professional and respectful manner.

---

## Expected Behavior

All contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication  
- Use inclusive and professional language  
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully  
- Collaborate in a positive and open-minded way  
- Focus on what is best for the project and its community  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments  
- Personal attacks or disrespectful language  
- Hate speech or exclusion based on identity  
- Sharing inappropriate or harmful content  
- Any behavior that could harm contributors or communities  

---

## Data Ethics and Responsibility

Lugayetu involves the collection of **linguistic and voice data from native speakers**.  
All contributors must respect ethical standards when working with this data.

This includes:

- Respecting the dignity and privacy of contributors  
- Not using data for harmful or exploitative purposes  
- Acknowledging the cultural value of the languages involved  
- Ensuring responsible use of datasets  

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may take appropriate actions in response to unacceptable behavior, including:

- Warning the contributor  
- Removing contributions  
- Restricting or banning access to the project  

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

All reports will be handled seriously and confidentially.

---

## Acknowledgment

By participating in Lugayetu, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

---

**Lugayetu** aims to build not only technology, but also a respectful and ethical community around Congolese languages.
